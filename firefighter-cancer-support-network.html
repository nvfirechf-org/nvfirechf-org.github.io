Firefighter Cancer Support Network

Implement these 7 cancer prevention procedures to protect yourself and your coworkers:

  • Clean or change-out your PPE after every working fire.
  • After every fire, take a shower and change your clothing ASAP.
  • Never wear or place dirty PPE in living areas, including your car.
  • Consider sunscreen and wear a hat while working in the sun
  • Exhaust is deadly, be sure to use your station's exhaust system.
  • Rembember to get annual physicals, including cancer screenings!
  • SCBA's must be worn from entry thru overhaul, no exceptions!
For more info call (866) 994-3276 or visit firefightercancersupport.org
