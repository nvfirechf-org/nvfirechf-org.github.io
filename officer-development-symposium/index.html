NFCA Fire Officer III Training - November 7 & 8, 2019 (Reno, NV)

Introduction

The Fire Officer III Training program is being hosted by the Nevada Fire Chiefs Association with the support of the Nevada State Fire Marshal’s Office and our media partner, Jones & Bartlett Learning. This training will be focused on the Job Performance Requirement (JPRs) that are described in the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) 1021: Standard for Fire Officer Professional Qualifications, Chapters 4 - 7. Special emphasis will be placed on assisting fire officers with obtaining NFPA Fire Officer III certification.

Location

For 2019, the location for the NFCA Fire Officer III (FO III) training program will be at the Regional Public Safety Training Center at 5190 Spectrum Blvd., Reno, NV 89512.

Program Description

The course curriculum is based on Jones & Bartlett’s book Chief Officer: Principles and Practice – Second Edition. The book and training materials have been endorsed by the International Association of Fire Chiefs and the National Fire Protection Association. Participants will receive online access to the E-Book and course materials in advance of the class and will receive a hard copy of the text onsite. Completion of all the Fire Officer III classes meets the NFPA 1021 Standard regarding the “knowledge” requirement. To meet the “skills” requirement participants will receive a Nevada State Fire Marshal’s Office Task Book with the JPRs that will need to be signed off and returned to the NFCA within one-year to receive a Fire Officer III certificate issued by the NFCA. The Nevada State Fire Marshals’ Office will issue Fire Officer III certification to all Nevada fire personnel that complete the Fire Officer III training program, submit the JPR task book, and have a letter from their fire chief recommending FO III certification. Attendees from outside of Nevada are advised to consult the Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) in their State regarding Fire Officer III certification.

There are a total of 16 hours of classroom instruction. Classroom instruction will be provided by a variety of seasoned Chief Fire Officers from Nevada and surrounding states, including Fire Chiefs David Cochran, Shawn White, Rich Harvey, Jeff Bucanan and others. In addition to the classroom presentations, fire service-related vendors will be onsite showcasing their products and services. Coffee breaks and lunch will be provided both days.
