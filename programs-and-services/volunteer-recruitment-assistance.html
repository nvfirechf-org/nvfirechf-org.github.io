Volunteer Recruitment Assistance

Nevada is made up of many small towns, where volunteer firefighters are essential to maintaining the safety of each community. The Nevada Chiefs' goal is to make volunteer recruitment easier and less expensive for member fire departments.

We started a new volunteer recruitment program early in 2017, where community members located anywhere in Nevada can search for and apply online to become a volunteer firefighter. Community members can choose to apply to an individual fire department or apply to all fire departments near their home that has a need for volunteers. Applications are sent to the NFCA, where we forward applications to fire administrators at applicable fire departments. This volunteer recruitment program is free to all Nevada fire departments and in our first year, over 500 volunteer applicants were submitted through our Website.

  • We sponsors all Nevada fire department's use of FireDepartment.net, where fire departments can easily update fire department information and connect with a larger audience. Nevada Chiefs has also helped add an online volunteer firefighter applications to all NV volunteer & mostly-volunteer fire department profiles on firedepartment.net.
  • NFCA funds & manages multiple online marketing campaigns to encourage community members to become a volunteer firefighter.

Tips to Get More Volunteer Firefighter Applications

Having a central volunteer recruitment website for the entire state of Nevada (@ nvfirechf.org/volunteer), for community members to search for and apply to become volunteers, has been the key to the growth in volunteer applications. As an individual fire department there are a few things you can do to utilize this program to get more volunteer firefighter applications from people in your community:

  • Add a new page to you fire department's website titled "Volunteer Firefighters Needed" OR "Volunteer at {Fire Department Name}" and write about how your fire department needs for volunteers, describe your volunteer program and ask your community members consider becoming a volunteer firefighter to help keep the community safe.
  • On this page, add a link to the Nevada Fire Chiefs Volunteer recruitment program by using the following code:
  • By promoting the Nevada Chiefs state-wide volunteer program, you will gain the ability for potential volunteers to apply while online and your link also encourage search engines to send more people in your community to the Nevada Fire Chiefs website when they search for "Volunteer Opportunities".
  • The best way to get more volunteer applications is to put a compelling message (and volunteer application) in front of potential volunteers. While your fire department may already have it's own website (and/or social media page), people will only see those pages if they seek it out. By adding a message to other local websites (with other topics) and explaining the need for local volunteer firefighters, you'll reach a larger group of potential volunteers. In many cases, the visitors to other local websites may never see the fire department's website or social media page.
  • Ask your town/city, county, local news papers, local TV stations and other popular community oriented websites to either write a story about volunteer firefighting or write a short paragraph describing how your community's safety depends on volunteers at local fire departments. Then, provide a link to your fire department's volunteer page (or the state-wide volunteer firefighter application)
