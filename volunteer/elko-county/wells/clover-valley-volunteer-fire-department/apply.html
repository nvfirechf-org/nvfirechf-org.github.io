Volunteer Application - Clover Valley Volunteer Fire Department

Thank you for your interest in volunteering at Clover Valley Volunteer Fire Department.

This is a generic volunteer firefighter application for applying to any fire department in Nevada. After submitting your application, the fire department you're applied to may contact you for additional information..

REQUIREMENTS: Volunteer fire departments generally require you be 18 years old, have a strong desire to keep the community safe and commit to volunteering a minimum of 20 to 24 hrs a month. Typically no experience is necessary and the fire department provides the training.

Firefighting is not an easy job. As firefighters, we share the responsibility to keep the community safe 24/7. With this responsibility comes a great deal of satisfaction in being there for your fellow community members when they need help, the camaraderie you build working on a fire crew and by making new friends in the community.

If you're ready to become a volunteer firefighter, simply fill out the application below and the Nevada Fire Chiefs Association will forward your information to the Fire Department for consideration.
