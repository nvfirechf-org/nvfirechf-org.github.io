Become a Volunteer Firefighter

Volunteering is a rewarding way to get involved in your community. As a volunteer firefighter, you'll meet new people, gain new skills, help your neighbors and gain experience that can help develop your resume. Nevada Fire Chiefs Association provides this statewide volunteer firefighter application to all Nevada fire departments recruiting volunteers.

To apply, select a county where you'd like to become a volunteer firefighter. Then select a fire department from the list of fire departments that are activly recruiting volunteers. Applicants can also fill out a multi fire department volunteer firefighter application and your application will be distributed to all fire departments in your who area seeking volunteers.
