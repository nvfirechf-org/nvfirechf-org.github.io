Volunteer Recruitment Information
North Lyon County Fire Protection District is accepting volunteer applications

North Lyon County Fire Protection District is a career fire department. This means that our community's safety depends on people like you contributing their time as volunteers. Please, help the Fernley community by applying to become a volunteer firefighter.
